ABOUT

Welcome to Cross Country Construction Limited

17 Years Experience

CrossCountry Construction Ltd is an ongoing independent ready mixed concrete & Construction Company in Bangladesh. The company was established in 2009 with participation from The Charm Ltd, Revenco, MPCCL & TREL.

The company manufactures and supplies Ready Mix Concrete (RMC) and Construction Service. Its portfolio of customers includes construction companies, real estate developers, contractors, infrastructure companies, Industrial Projects (Cement, Steel, Power Plants, Amusement Park, Resorts, Hotel Building etc), Commercial Projects and individuals owners.CCCL also produces special concrete solutions using its world class infrastructure and technology and ensures product and service quality.

Over the years, CCCL has established a reputation for being able to deliver cost-effective and innovative Construction solutions evolving nature of the industry, CCCL has consistently invested in building and maintaining state-of-the-art R&D, manufacturing and quality control infrastructure. It is this focus on quality and innovation that has enabled CCCL to build a client base that includes some of the most respected names from the Bangladesh Ready Mix Concrete (RMC) and construction sector.

Having captured a significant share of the market in many parts of Bangladesh, CCCL is now spreading its network to other locations in Bangladesh to serve the requirements of the rapidly growing Bangladesh’s construction industry.

CCCL is specially known for Construction Project based In-site Concrete Batching Plant (Ready Mix Concrete) services.
