History And Milestones

CrossCountry Construction Ltd is an ongoing independent ready mixed concrete & Construction Company in Bangladesh. The company was established in 2009 with participation from The Charm Ltd, Revenco, MPCCL & TREL.

The company manufactures and supplies Ready Mix Concrete (RMC) and Construction Service. Its portfolio of customers includes construction companies, real estate developers, contractors, infrastructure companies, Industrial Projects (Cement, Steel, Power Plants, Amusement Park, Resorts, Hotel Building etc), Commercial Projects and individuals owners. Apart from normal concrete mixes of various grades, CCCL also produces special concrete solutions using its world class infrastructure and technology and ensures product and service quality.
